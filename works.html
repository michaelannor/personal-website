Design and Development

The Cobalt Partners Website

This involved a redesign of the company's website to conform with its new corporate identity. Designing this website was a very iterative process with several directions and sketches considered. The final design was done using Sketch and developed using Foundation and Jekyll


Amplify Pay Platfrom Redesign

This project involved a complete overhaul of the payment platform and the corporate website and involved creating personas, user and process flows, low medium and high fidelity prototypes in order to create a new design specification to be developed.


michaelannor.design

The plan here was to design and develop a simple portfolio site in as little time as possible. But I needed something that clearly portrayed me as a person. I threw in a few sketches on paper to decide on a general idea and began developing higher fidelity designs using Sketch. Once the concept fully evolved and the design was ready, I put HTML, CSS and Javascript together and this is what we have.

Research Projects

A Lightweight Implementation of the Internet of Things (IoT)

The Internet has undergone many changes in its 45-year-old history. The next wave for the Internet is the Internet of Things (IoT). This project implements a lightweight IoT system that can control devices in the home. This system can then be extended to other use cases in agriculture, healthcare and education.


Scoop News - News Reinvented

Led a team of course mates on an experiment to design and prototype a novel way of channelling relevant and targeted news to a population with an array of mobile and social media tools. Designed and Developed an HTML/CSS prototype and tested it with a sample population.
